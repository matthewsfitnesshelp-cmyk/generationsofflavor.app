Privacy Policy

Generations of Flavor — Last updated: February 2026

Matthews Fitness LLC ("we", "our", "us") operates the Generations of Flavor mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you use Generations of Flavor, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • User Content: Recipes, photos, notes, and other content you create or upload within the App.
  • Family Data: Family group membership, invite codes, and your role within family groups.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, including features used and recipes viewed.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App's functionality, including recipe sharing and family collaboration features.
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • To enable family cookbook features, including sharing recipes and managing group membership.
  • To send you notifications related to your family cookbooks (e.g., new recipes, member requests).
  • To improve the App and develop new features.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, which employs industry-standard encryption and security practices. Recipe images are stored in Firebase Cloud Storage. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information, but no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data may be shared in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within Your Families: Recipes and profile information you contribute are visible to members of your family groups.
  • Service Providers: We use Google Firebase for authentication, data storage, and cloud functions. Google's privacy practices govern their handling of data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights.

Your Rights and Choices

  • You can view and edit your profile information within the App.
  • You can leave family groups at any time.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • You can manage notification permissions through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Generations of Flavor is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by other means. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
